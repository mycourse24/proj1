Crypto trading

What is crypto trading

Crypto trading is the act of buying and selling digital assets, like Bitcoin or Ethereum, on an exchange to profit from price fluctuations. Traders purchase a cryptocurrency when they believe its value will rise and sell it later for a profit, or they can speculate on a price decrease.

Technical Analysis

Technical analysis is a means of examining and predicting price movements in the financial markets, by using historical price charts and market statistics.
